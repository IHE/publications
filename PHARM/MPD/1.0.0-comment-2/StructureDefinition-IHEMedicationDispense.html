Resource Profile: IHE Medication Dispense

Official URL: https://profiles.ihe.net/PHARM/MPD/StructureDefinition/IHEMedicationDispense Version: 1.0.0-comment-2
Active as of 2025-05-27 Computable Name: IHEMedicationDispense

Copyright/Legal: IHE http://www.ihe.net/Governance/#Intellectual_Property

MedicationDispense profile for capturing dispensation information based on a medication prescription.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: MedicationDispense/10-dispense-1, MedicationDispense/10-dispense-2 and MedicationDispense/300-dispense-for-2-requests
  • CapabilityStatements using this Resource Profile: IHE MPD Dispense Consumer Actor and IHE MPD Dispense Reporter Actor (server)
