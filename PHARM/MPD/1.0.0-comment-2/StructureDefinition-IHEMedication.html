Resource Profile: IHE Medication

Official URL: https://profiles.ihe.net/PHARM/MPD/StructureDefinition/IHEMedication Version: 1.0.0-comment-2
Active as of 2025-05-27 Computable Name: IHEMedication

Copyright/Legal: IHE http://www.ihe.net/Governance/#Intellectual_Property

Medication for clinical use cases. This could be a branded product or a generic concept, also allowing describing pack size and components of complex products.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: IHE Medication, IHE Medication Dispense and IHE Medication Order
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Medication/01A-Cefuroxime1500GenericExplicit, Medication/01B-Cefuroxime1500GenericConcept, Medication/01C-Cefuroxime1500Branded, Medication/01D-Cefuroxime750Branded... Show 6 more, Medication/02A-ClotrimazoleCanifugCremolum, Medication/02A1-CanifugCremolumCreamItem, Medication/02A2-CanifugCremolumPessaryItem, Medication/03B-VitaminBComplexBranded, Medication/04-FirmagonBranded and Medication/05A-Tilidin-Branded
