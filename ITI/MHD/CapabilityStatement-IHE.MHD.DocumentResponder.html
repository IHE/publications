IHE ITI Mobile access to Health Documents (MHD) - Document Responder (server).

This is the Requirements CapabilityStatement for a Document Responder. The Document Responder may declared the XDS on FHIR Option or the UnContained References Options. This Actor Shall provide server implementing Find Document List ITI-66, Find Document References ITI-67, and Retrieve Document ITI-68 transactions.
