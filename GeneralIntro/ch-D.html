Appendix D: Glossary

IHE Glossary terms are maintained via the Standards Knowledge Management Tool (SKMT). The SKMT contains terms from Final Text and Trial Implementation IHE profiles. New terms introduced in Public Comment profiles (or terms that have been modified) are added once the profile is published for Trial Implementation. The following list is current as of the date referenced. Always refer to the SKMT for the most current list of IHE Glossary terms. See the IHE Wiki for further information about accessing the SKMT.
