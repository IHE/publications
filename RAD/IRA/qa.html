introductionThe Integrated Reporting Applications (IRA) profile helps applications that are used together during reporting (e.g., image display, report creator, clinical applications, AI tools, etc) to share information using a standard called FHIRcast. Other applications are notified so they can then intelligently synchronize their behavior or use the new data.
