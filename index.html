IHE Publications

The IHE Technical Frameworks are a resource for users, developers and implementers of healthcare imaging and information systems. They define specific implementations of established standards to achieve effective systems integration, facilitate appropriate sharing of medical information and support optimal patient care. Technical Framework documents are maintained regularly by the IHE Technical Committees through the identification and correction of errata. Each IHE International Domains's Technical Frameworks, Technical Framework Supplements, and other supporting material are available at the links provided below.

IHE Technical Frameworks General Introduction and Shared Appendices

The IHE Technical Frameworks General Introduction and Shared Appendices are components shared by all of IHE domain Technical Frameworks. They are intended to provide context and conventions for those documents. This information should be read in companion with a specific domain's Technical Framework.
