Resource Profile: CCG Data-In Bundle IPS CarePlan Specific Resource

Official URL: https://profiles.ihe.net/QRPH/CCG/StructureDefinition/ccg-data-in-ips-careplan Version: 1.0.0-comment
Active as of 2025-05-20 Computable Name: CCG_Data_In_IPS_CarePlan

This is a CarePlan profile that forces at least one of the activity.definitionCanonical to be a CARD Folder

Usage:

  • Use this Resource Profile: CCG IPS Bundle
