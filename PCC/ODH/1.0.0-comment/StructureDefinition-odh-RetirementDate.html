Resource Profile: Retirement Date

Official URL: https://profiles.ihe.net/PCC/ODH/StructureDefinition/odh-RetirementDate Version: 1.0.0-comment
Active as of 2024-03-08 Computable Name: RetirementDate

A Date of Retirement Observation is a clinical statement about a self-reported date (at least year) that a person considers themselves to have ‘retired’. If present, the person has retired. A person can be both retired and employed; most often, a person would be retired from one job and employed in another job.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: Occupational Data For Health
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Observation/observation-odh-retirement-date-sample
