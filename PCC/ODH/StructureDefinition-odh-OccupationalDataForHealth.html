Resource Profile: Occupational Data For Health

Official URL: https://profiles.ihe.net/PCC/ODH/StructureDefinition/odh-OccupationalDataForHealth Version: 1.0.0
Draft as of 2024-12-04 Computable Name: OccupationalDataForHealth
Other Identifiers: OID:1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.43.42.4

This Composition covers information about a patient's work, including some voluntary work, or a patient's household members' work. ODH is designed for the social history section of a medical record, to facilitate clinical care in multiple disciplines and delivery environments. ODH can be used for clinical decision support, population health activities and value-based care, and public health reporting.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Composition/composition-odh-occupational-data-for-health-sample
